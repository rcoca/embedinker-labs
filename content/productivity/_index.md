A space dedicated to tracking personal organization as an engineering problem, focusing on modular, "Lego-like" systems designed to survive real-world chaos.

Rather than enforcing a rigid, fragile routine, this framework is built to scale dynamically based on your available cognitive bandwidth—and, crucially, to degrade gracefully when things catch fire.

### Core Architecture:

* **Layered Resolution:** Build and stack tracking mechanisms up to the exact level of detail required for deep architectural sprints or complex multi-project roadmaps. 
* **The Minimalist Fallback:** When chaotic periods hit, a dense system becomes a liability and collapses under its own administrative overhead. This framework includes an explicit, bare-minimum fallback layer—a high-resilience "safe mode" that strips away all complexity but ensures your core state and critical paths are maintained with near-zero friction.
* **Frictionless Recovery:** Once the storm passes, the modular nature of the system allows you to easily scale back up to higher resolutions without having to rebuild your tracking state from scratch.
